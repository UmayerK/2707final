Watch Materials Guide

Case Materials

  • Stainless Steel: Durable and corrosion-resistant
  • 18K Gold: Available in yellow, rose, and white
  • Platinum: Premium and hypoallergenic
  • Titanium: Lightweight and strong
  • Ceramic: Scratch-resistant and modern

Crystal Types

  • Sapphire Crystal: Extremely scratch-resistant
  • Mineral Glass: Durable and cost-effective
  • Acrylic: Impact-resistant and vintage style

Strap Options

  • Metal Bracelet: Classic and durable
  • Leather: Elegant and comfortable
  • Rubber: Sporty and water-resistant
  • NATO: Casual and versatile
