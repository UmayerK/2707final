Watch Sizing Guide

How to Measure Your Wrist

  1. Use a flexible measuring tape
  2. Wrap around the wrist where you wear your watch
  3. Allow for slight movement (add 1/4 inch)

Watch Case Sizes

  • Small: 34-36mm (ideal for slim wrists)
  • Medium: 37-39mm (versatile fit)
  • Large: 40-42mm (modern standard)
  • Extra Large: 43mm+ (statement pieces)
