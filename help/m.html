Watch Care & Maintenance Guide

Keep your timepiece in perfect condition

Daily Care

  • Wipe your watch with a soft, lint-free cloth daily
  • Keep away from magnets and electronic devices
  • Store in a watch box when not wearing
  • Avoid extreme temperatures
  • Remove watch before showering or swimming (unless water-resistant)

Water Resistance

  • Check water resistance rating before exposure to water
  • Have seals tested annually
  • Replace gaskets every 2-3 years
  • Ensure crown is fully screwed down before water exposure
  • Rinse with fresh water after salt water exposure

Professional Service

  • Service mechanical watches every 3-5 years
  • Replace battery in quartz watches as needed
  • Have water resistance tested annually
  • Clean and oil movement regularly
  • Inspect strap or bracelet condition

Storage Tips

  • Use a quality watch box or case
  • Keep in a cool, dry place
  • Avoid direct sunlight
  • Use silica gel packets to control humidity
  • Keep automatic watches on a winder when stored
