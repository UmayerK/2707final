A Legacy of Timeless Excellence

Founded in 1985 by master horologist Marcus Zeitgeist, our journey began in a small workshop in Geneva's historic watchmaking district.

What started as a passionate pursuit of perfection has evolved into one of the world's most respected luxury timepiece establishments, serving distinguished collectors and connoisseurs across the globe.

Our Heritage

For over three decades, we've been at the forefront of luxury timepiece curation. Our expertise spans from vintage masterpieces to contemporary innovations, each piece telling its own unique story of craftsmanship and excellence.

In 1992, we made history by restoring the legendary Celestial Chronograph for the Royal Collection, cementing our reputation as master craftsmen. Today, our restoration workshop handles fewer than 100 pieces annually, ensuring each timepiece receives the attention it deserves.
